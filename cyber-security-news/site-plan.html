Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform people about recent cyber security news. Examples would be new technology, data breaches, tricks to make yourself and your company more secure.

Audience

Anyone who would want knowledge of all of the recent cyber security news but don't want to look all over the internet and figure out what is true.

Dynamic elements

I am thinking of having something akin to the book review blog where there are multiple posts that go in order by date. A picture, headline, a synopsis of the story, and the date.
